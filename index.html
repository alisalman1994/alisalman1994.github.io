What we learn in PCEP

"The problem that we want to solve is the high incidence of medication errors in Iraq."

So we write articles about most important and common medical errors that we noticed or how professionally deal with different medical cases by attractive, short, directed writing manner. Then send them to the app users of the pharmacist and doctors or any healthcare providers hoping that they will use them for safer prescription to the public.

Our goals

our principle goal is protecting peoples from the bad medication use and so medication errors. We work on achieving our goals by :

Improve pharmacist knowledge

Ithe pharmacist is the cornerstone and the responsible of identification and correcting medication errors so he is our main target.

Provide easy learning

the app make the learning so easy and attractive. It also accessible simple by everyone.

Focus on common medication errors

We focus on the most common and dangerous medication errors that occur in Iraq.

Guidelines following encouragement

we review the guidelines of treating different medical conditions and send short notes about the most important aspect to users to encourage guidelines following.
